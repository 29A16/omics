## GeoMx
Both [[Spatial transcriptomics|mRNA]] and [[Spatial proteomics|proteins]] can be detected simultaneously with GeoMx. Both RNA probes and Ab carry oligonucleotide bar-codes, connected via UV-cleavable linkers. After binding of the probes in the Region of Interest (ROI), the bar-codes are released by scanning the tissue with a laser and sequenced to identify the type of tissue/expression of selected markers.
The shape of the ROI can be adjusted to take any desired geometry, but requires at least 20 cells for proeomic and 200 cells for transcriptomic studies.
