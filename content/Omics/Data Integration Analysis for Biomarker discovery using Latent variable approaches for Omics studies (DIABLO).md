DIABLO is a [[Supervised vs. Unsupervised multiomics|supervised]] method, that functions similarly to [[Multi omics factor analysis (MOFA)|MOFA]]. The factors of MOFA are called components and the output also contains information on the covariance between the groups and also a design matrix, that shows whether a condition correlates with another or not (and the strength of correlation 0-1).
