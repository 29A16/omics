The [[Spatial transcriptomics pre-processing]] requires image segmentation for image based techniques and the creation of expression-by cell matrix. Machine learning is the bases for most segmentation methods. Most of the software is proprietary and some of it can be retrained with your own data.
After completing the Segmentation, the cells can be [[Cell type annotation|annotated]].
