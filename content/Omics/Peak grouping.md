Since several peaks might correspond to the same metabolite - different isotopes, adducts or fragments, correct grouping is complicated. Although prior knowledge on the underlying chemical rules , as well as the elution profile helps.
Due to this complexity over fitting easily happens when to many analytes are added.

## Deconvolution
When considering which peak belongs to which metabolite, **co-variation** helps. If variation is introduced, the same behaviour is expected of identical compounds.
