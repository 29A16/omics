[[Differential gene expression - scRNAseq]]
Cells can be identified with specific markers, wither manually or automatic. Manually checking for the presence of specific markers (due to the low coverage of scRNAseq, some low abundance genes are still considered positives) in a cell cluster is transparent but slow and subjective, if no known markers are used.
Automatic annotation can either be conducted on individual cells or on clusters. Identification of individual cells is preferred but might not always be possible if information is missing. Known marker genes have to be compiled in a reference dataset that is similar to the analyte.
## Verification 
To verify results (especially unexpected ones) statistical methods can be employed as well as additional experiments and complementary omics.
