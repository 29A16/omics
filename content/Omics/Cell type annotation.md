Some underlying problems of annotating the cells are imperfect [[Image pre-processing|segmentation]], poor image quality and autofluorescence of the cells. Traditionally thresholds where manually selected — for this negative markers were sometimes included (markers not present in the sample), while today machine based labelling is mostly employed. The automatic labelling can either be unsupervised or training data can be provided for supervised methods.
