Multiomics integrates data from different omics experiments if they overlap. E.g. Proteotranscriptomics can use data from the sample, from which DNA, RNA, and Proteins are extracted sequentially. The generated transcriptomics and proteomics data can then be combined (note that the method DDA/DIA matters for their compatibility/overlap). A difference between the transcriptome and proteome can occur due to errors but might also have a biological reason.
