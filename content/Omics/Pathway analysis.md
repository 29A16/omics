The final step in [[Metabolomics - Data processing|processing metabolomics data]] is the mapping of identified metabolites (and metabolite levels) to their pathway.
The mapping suffers from some problems present in metabolomic research, like poor coverage, unknown background and a high uncertainty of the results. Another problem is presented by the lack of known pathways and method dependent biases (extraction, processing, analysis).
