PLS and [[Principal component analysis (PCA)|PCA]] are methods to reduce the dimensions of a given data set. PLS is a supervised method and considers the co-variance of different factors, e.g. metabolite level and biological response. It identifies the components, that are most likely to predict the specific response.
The final result is more fine structured than PCA.
