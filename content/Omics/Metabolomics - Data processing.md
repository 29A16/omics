#startingpoint 
As discussed [[Metabolomics Technology|before]] the generated MS data is multi dimensional (retention time, m/z, intensity) and highly complex. Mass- and retention tolerance of the machine complicate this even more. Therefore the settings for picking peaks have to be adjusted between machines and runs to identify the analysed metabolites and avoid false detection of metabolites, that are not present.
