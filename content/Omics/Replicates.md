To perform any statistical analysis replicates are required. **Technical replicates** are generally used to evaluate the performance of the analytical technique, while **Biological replicates** are required to find the biological effect. 
**Training/validation data** is also employed to give a point of reference to the measured data.
To assess the amount of replicates required for the experiment **Power calculations** can be used. In this calculation the hypothesis, statistical method, effect size and variation are considered.
The amount of replicates should generally increase with the amount of variables observed.
