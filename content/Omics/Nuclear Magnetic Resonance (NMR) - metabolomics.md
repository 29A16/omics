[[Analytical techniques]]
NMR is les commonly used for metabolomic analysis than MS, but has some distinct advantages. The main plus of employing NMR is its speed and the ability to give absolute values for the amount analytes.
What makes NMR unattractive for some metabolomic experiments is its poor sensitivity and low resolution. The amount of samples analysed is also quite low, when compared to the capabilities of MS (although very quick). 
A neat trick for the identification of complex samples is to utilize their co-variation (adding a specific metabolite to the sample will increase the hight of the corresponding peak in the spectra)
