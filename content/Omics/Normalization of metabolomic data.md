[[Steps in data processing (MS)]]
Normalization can be carried out with different features to normalize to, that all carry their own disadvantages. Normalization to the **total spectral are** (AUC) introduces a bias towards the most abundant metabolites. **Creatine** is a common marker in urine samples but also varies with age, sex, and lifestyle. Using **internal markers** might not reflect the variation of the analysed samples.

Therefore, precise extraction and preparation of the samples is very important, when different samples are compared.
