The method for [[2 Thoughts/Metabolomics|metabolomic analysis]] depends on the aim. There are different approaches when specific metabolite(s) are identified or pathways are investigated. 
Metabolomics can also be used to characterize a disease or study the **aexposome** (response to exposure to specific chemical).
## Targeted and untargeted approaches
Traditionally more targeted approcches where chosen to identify specific metabolites or groups of metabolites (e.g. for diagnostic purposes). More and more untargted approaches are choosen, where the whole (or most of the) metabolome is measured. Some related terms are defined below.
>[!cite] Metabolic fingerprinting
>*The profile patterns of samples are compared with each other to identify shifts in the global metabolite composition (the identity of single metabolites is not important)*

>[!cite] Metabolic profiling
>*~untargeted approach, whereas many metabolites as possible are detected (most common method)*

>[!cite] Metabolic flux analysis
>*Is able to distinguish between the accumulation or increase in flux of a metabolite*
