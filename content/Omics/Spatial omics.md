#startingpoint 
Spatial omics among others uses transcriptomic or proteomic techniques (and their combination) and applies them to identify cell types, their interactions, and transcription profile in tissues.
In contrast to simple staining techniques or immunofluorescence spatial omics can utilize sequencing techniques to identify cellular characteristics (e.g. for cancer).
The main application discussed here is the Tumour micro environment (TMA) and the interaction (/inactivation) with immune cells.
