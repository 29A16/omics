There is a high variation between individuals, that has to be taken into account when evaluating metabolomic data [[Statistical analysis|statistically]]. To accurately compare the response of individuals, e.g. to a treatment, the same individual has to be compared to themselves (used as their own control). The same pairing can also be considered for other factors with high variation like experiment day or batch.![[Metabolomics3.pdf#page=24&rect=114,36,741,252|Metabolomics3, p.24]]
