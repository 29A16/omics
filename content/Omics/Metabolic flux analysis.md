The [[Metabolomics Technology|metabolomic techniques]] described here are only able to study the metabolites of a sample taken at a specific point in time — they are steady state experiments.
Due to the high variability of the metabolome the metabolite turnover/flux is of much higher relevance than the profile at a specific time point.
NMR can be used due to its high efficiency for flux analyses in conjunction with **tracers**. Traditionally radioactive tracers were used but stable isotopes are also available now.
