OMICS: "[[L12 Spatial omics]]"
---
The goal of upstream analysis in [[Spatial omics]] is a cell by feature matrix, that includes the location of the cell. The downstream analysis also includes data in differential expression and cell-cell interaction. It can also include other data like clinical information.
This is highly specific on the technology/data and commercial software is often employed.
