## 3D data structure
GC/LC-MS data typically consists of three dimensions — time, m/z and peak intensity. The 3D plot can be broken down into different views for analyses:
- The mass spectrum shows intensity and m/z ratio at a given time point
- The total ion current sums up all intensities over time
- And the extracted ion chromatogram reveals the intensity over time for one set m/z value.
